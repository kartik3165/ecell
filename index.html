Entrepreneurial Fest 2024

Igniting Innovation, Celebrating Ideas

About the Event

The **Entrepreneurial Fest 2024** is a 3-day event dedicated to igniting the spirit of entrepreneurship among students. With activities ranging from pitch competitions to seminars, workshops, and an entertainment night, this event aims to foster creativity, collaboration, and innovation. The fest brings together students, professionals, and thought leaders to create opportunities, learn new skills, and enjoy memorable experiences.

Event Highlights

Day Event Time Description
Day 1 Pitching Competition 10:00 AM Participants will pitch their innovative ideas to a panel of industry experts. This competition offers the opportunity for networking and potential mentorship from experienced entrepreneurs.
Day 1 Seminar on Innovation 2:00 PM A seminar led by thought leaders discussing how innovation drives business success. Learn key strategies to cultivate creativity and innovation in the entrepreneurial journey.
Day 2 Workshop on Startup Scaling 11:00 AM An interactive workshop focused on the challenges and solutions for scaling a startup. Engage with experts to understand the nuances of growth strategies.
Day 2 Networking Lunch 1:00 PM Join fellow attendees, entrepreneurs, and speakers for a networking lunch. A great opportunity to connect, collaborate, and exchange ideas in a casual setting.
Day 3 Pitching Competition Finals 10:00 AM The final round of the Pitching Competition where the top ideas are presented for a chance to win exciting prizes and mentorship opportunities.
Day 3 Entertainment Night 7:00 PM The fest concludes with an Entertainment Night filled with music, performances, and fun activities to relax and celebrate the achievements of the event.
